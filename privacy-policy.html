Near Miss Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03/03/2025

NearMiss is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information to ensure transparency and trust.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and employee ID (if applicable).
  • Incident Data: Reports including incident name, severity, description, location, date/time, and uploaded media.
  • Device & Usage Information: Log data, IP address, and device details for analytics and service improvements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Process and manage incident reports efficiently.
  • Notify relevant departments for timely resolution.
  • Improve app functionality, security, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal requirements and industry regulations.

3. Data Sharing & Security

Your privacy is our priority. We ensure:

  • We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Incident data is shared only with authorized personnel within your organization.
  • Robust security measures are implemented to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to, modify, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the app.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related inquiries.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of our services after changes implies acceptance of the revised terms.
